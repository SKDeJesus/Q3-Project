Walker Scobell

All The Tea presents you, one of our newest yet already succesful child actors, Walker Scobell.


Scobell, also know as 'Mini Ryan Reynolds' by the internet, landed his very first role which was The Adam Project (more details below) and from there his acting career skyrocketed.

This American 15 year old is not the only gifted one in his family though, he has two siblings, Leena and Tanner Scobell, whom are background characters in a series hes starring in as the main, Percy Jackson and the Olympians.

So far he has been in 3 films (click Films to see detailed information about them), two of which I have already mentioned and lastly, Secret Headquarters, with him as the main character once more alongside Momona Tamada and Abby Witherspoon, very good friends of his.

He is also popular amongst the other child actors and actresses, in other words he's friends with a lot of the other famous names we know today.

Not only that but his career started at the age of 12 which is very young, younger than most to be honest.
